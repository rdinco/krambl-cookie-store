KRAMBL COOKIE STORE - START HERE

1. Copy this whole folder to:
   C:\xampp\htdocs\krambl-cookie-store

2. Start Apache and MySQL in XAMPP.

3. Open:
   http://localhost/phpmyadmin

4. Click Import and choose:
   database/krambl_store.sql

5. Open the website:
   http://localhost/krambl-cookie-store
