CHAPTER 6 - SOURCE CODE FILE GUIDE

config.php
Purpose: Starts the session and connects the website to MySQL using mysqli_connect().

includes/functions.php
Purpose: Contains simple reusable functions for login checking, cart counting, money display, and audit logging.

index.php
Purpose: Displays the homepage and featured products.

register.php
Purpose: Registers buyers and creates an email verification link.

verify.php
Purpose: Confirms the buyer's email account.

login.php
Purpose: Checks the user's email and password and starts a login session.

logout.php
Purpose: Ends the user's login session.

store.php
Purpose: Displays categorized products and provides a simple search function.

cart.php
Purpose: Adds products, updates quantities, removes products, and calculates the total.

checkout.php
Purpose: Collects and confirms the customer's delivery details.

payment.php
Purpose: Displays demo payment choices, saves the order, and reduces stock.

about.php
Purpose: Displays company and group-member information.

admin/dashboard.php
Purpose: Displays the seller dashboard.

admin/users.php
Purpose: Adds, edits, and deletes administrator accounts.

admin/products.php
Purpose: Adds, edits, deletes, prices, and updates product stocks.

admin/inventory.php
Purpose: Displays the remaining inventory report.

admin/audit.php
Purpose: Displays activities completed by logged-in users.
